HOW TO PLAY

Jujutsu Chess — Tactical Ritual
⚔ The Basics

Jujutsu Chess is a 1-vs-1 chess variant where each side wields cursed techniques alongside standard chess moves. You play as White against an AI opponent.

Win by checkmating the enemy King — or outlasting them with superior technique use. Standard chess rules apply: legal moves, check, checkmate, en passant, castling, and pawn promotion.

⚡ Cursed Energy (CE)

Both sides start with a fixed CE pool. CE does not regenerate passively — it is a finite resource you must spend wisely. Skills cost CE to activate — the cost is shown on each skill button.

  • Your CE bar is shown in blue; the enemy's in red.
  • Your CE pool starts at the highest ELO you have defeated (minimum 300). Beating harder bots increases your pool permanently.
  • Certain vows (Hunger, Divine, Sacrifice) modify your starting CE.
  • Six Eyes passive halves all skill costs for its holder.
  • Black Flash — any capture has a 10% chance to grant 200 CE to the capturing side.
🔮 Skills & Techniques

Skills are unlocked by defeating opponents and equipped in the Technique Archive. You can equip up to 9 skill slots (4 Special, 2 Ability, 1 Domain, 1 RCT, 1 Ultimate). During battle, click a skill button to activate it.

SPECIAL SLOTS (S1–S4)

Divergent Fist (20 CE) — Click an enemy pawn to destroy it and leave a cursed hazard on that square. Blocked by Infinity and Limitless.
Divine Dog (80 CE) — Summons a shikigami wolf (Knight + Bishop moves). Click again to retract. Cannot be re-summoned if destroyed.
Straw Doll (60 CE) — Destroys any non-pawn enemy piece within line of sight. Cannot harm Mahoraga.
Reversal Red (150 CE) — Destroys any enemy piece (including pawns) within line of sight. Cannot harm Mahoraga.
Cleave (140 CE) — Destroys any enemy piece (not King, not Mahoraga) anywhere on the board. No line-of-sight needed.
Lapse Blue (60 CE) — Select any piece (not King), then click any square to teleport it there instantly, capturing what's there. Bypasses Infinity/Limitless.
Dismantle (200 CE) — Click an enemy piece; destroys all enemy pieces in a 3-wide horizontal sweep (col-1, col, col+1) on that row. Requires column line-of-sight.
Cursed Speech (80 CE) — Seals one random opponent skill for 20 moves. Once per battle only.
Copy (100 CE) — Copies one random skill from the opponent for a single use. Once per battle.
Nue (120 CE) — Summons the shikigami Nue (Bishop that can also teleport to any empty square). Cannot interact with King. Cannot be re-summoned if destroyed.
Heian Cleave (220 CE) — Auto-destroys the 2 most valuable enemy pieces simultaneously. No targeting needed.
Heian Dismantle (380 CE) — Destroys all enemy pieces in a 5-wide horizontal sweep (col-2 to col+2). Requires column line-of-sight.

ABILITY SLOTS (A1–A2)

Six Eyes (passive) — Halves ALL your skill costs permanently, including Limitless triggers.
Limitless (passive) — Auto-spends 50 CE per enemy attack to block it. Makes all pieces immortal for one turn. Mahoraga adapts after 3 blocks.
Infinity (120 CE) — Blocks ALL enemy attacks (chess and skills) until your next turn. Mahoraga (adapted) and Heavenly Restriction can pierce it.
Mahoraga (200 CE) — Summons the Divine General. Adapts to techniques used against it. Only Hollow Purple, Fuga, and chess captures can destroy it.
Heavenly Restriction (passive) — King moves like a Queen. Move two pieces per turn. Bypasses all Infinity/Limitless. Seals all other slots.
Projection Sorcery (passive, 80 CE/turn) — +1 extra move per turn. CE drains at turn start; skipped if insufficient.
Rika (150 CE) — Equipping Rika doubles your starting CE pool. Summons Rika as a bonus Queen; while on the board halves all skill costs. Retract free. Cannot be re-summoned if captured.
Idle Transfiguration (150 CE) — Click one of your pieces (not King/Queen) to instantly promote it to Queen. Does not consume your turn. (Moved to Special slot)
Imaginary Fierce God (passive) — Extra front row R N B Q Q B N R spawns at battle start. Also grants +1 move per turn.

RCT SLOT

Reverse Cursed Technique (80 CE) — Restores your most recently lost piece onto the board. Does not trigger Mahoraga adaptation.

ULTIMATE SLOT

Hollow Purple (300 CE) — Click any column to annihilate it. With Annihilation vow: destroys 3 columns (center ±1). Can destroy Mahoraga and Rika.
World Cutting Slash (free, 4 activations) — 4-press incantation: Scale of the Dragon → Recoil → Twin Meteors → SLASH. On 4th press destroys the nearest enemy in every column simultaneously.
Fuga (500 CE) — Requires Malevolent Shrine: Heian domain active. Click a center square to annihilate all enemy pieces in a 5×5 area. Cannot hit King.
Hollow Nuke (free, 4 presses) — Gojo's final convergence. An 8-stage incantation consumed 2 stages per press: Phase → Twilight → Eyes of Wisdom → Nine Ropes → Polarized Light → Crow and Declaration → Between the Front and Back → HOLLOW PURPLE. On the 4th press, all enemy pieces on the inner board (columns B–G, ranks 2–7) are annihilated — King, Mahoraga, and Rika included. Pieces on files A/H and ranks 1/8 (the border) survive. Unlock by defeating Gojo Satoru (Strongest).

DOMAIN SLOTS

Malevolent Shrine (500 CE) — Destroys 2 most valuable enemy pieces every 3 turns. Overwhelms all other domains.
Infinite Void (500 CE) — Seals enemy for 10 turns. Enemy Mahoraga collapses it instantly. Overwhelms all other domains except Malevolent Shrine.
True Mutual Love (500 CE) — Each turn a random skill from the entire pool fires against the enemy automatically.
Self Embodiment of Perfection (500 CE) — Warps enemy pieces into pawns each turn; once all are pawns, pawns disappear.
Time Cell Moon Palace (500 CE) — Free +1 move per turn; enemy pieces that move are destroyed. Collapses against Void/Shrine.
Chimera Shadow Garden (500 CE) — Shadow clones of your pieces appear as new pieces each turn for 10 turns.
Malevolent Shrine: Heian (800 CE) — True form of Shrine. Destroys 3 most valuable pieces every 2 turns. Overwhelms all domains.
🌐 Domain Expansion & Clashes

Domain skills transform the entire battlefield. When two domains are active simultaneously, a Domain Clash occurs:

  • Both domains' effects are suppressed for 20 turns.
  • Neither side can use any skill during a clash — only chess moves are allowed.
  • Check-win: if you put the enemy King in check during a clash, their domain collapses and yours takes over immediately.
  • Domains with Infinite Void or Malevolent Shrine overpower all others — no clash, just instant collapse.

Each domain clash has a unique visual: pink, gold, purple, crimson, depending on which domains are fighting.

☸ Mahoraga & Adaptation

Mahoraga is unique — it adapts to techniques used against it:

  • After blocking Infinity or Limitless 3 times, Mahoraga pierces through them permanently.
  • If an active domain is on the board while Mahoraga is present, Mahoraga's wheel spins each turn. After 3 turns, it collapses the domain entirely.
  • Destroying Mahoraga seals the Ten Shadows — it cannot be resummoned.
∞ Infinity & Limitless

Infinity (active, 120 CE): blocks ALL enemy attacks — both chess captures and all attacking skills — until the start of your next turn.

Limitless (passive): automatically spends 50 CE to block each enemy attack. Makes all your pieces immune for the entire turn. Unlike Infinity, it can run out of CE.

Both are disabled during a Domain Clash. Lapse Blue bypasses both entirely.

⚠ Infinity/Limitless Bypass Rules

No skill or attack can pierce Infinity or Limitless by default. The only ways to bypass them are:

  • A — Active domain: if the attacker has their own domain currently active, all their skills and attacks ignore Infinity/Limitless.
  • B — Mahoraga adapted: if Mahoraga has adapted to Infinity or Limitless, every skill and attack used by Mahoraga's owner permanently pierces through both barriers.
  • C — Domain Clash: during an active domain clash, Infinity and Limitless are fully suppressed for both sides.
  • D — Heavenly Restriction: a combatant with Heavenly Restriction (Zenin Toji and any player equipping that skill) ignores both barriers entirely.

If none of conditions A–D apply, Infinity and Limitless are absolute — no skill in the game can break them.

📜 Binding Vows

Before each battle you can choose a Binding Vow — a risk/reward pact that modifies the rules of combat:

  • Annihilation — all offensive skills enhanced (Hollow Purple 3 cols, Cleave hits 2 pieces, etc.), but starting CE −200.
  • Speed — gain +1 extra chess move per turn, but all skill costs +50%.
  • Hunger — every piece you capture restores 100× its value in CE (massive snowball), but start with 0 CE.
  • Sacrifice — press X on any selected non-King piece to sacrifice it for 50× value CE (once per turn), but Black Flash is disabled.
  • Divine — starting CE ×3 and all cooldowns start at zero, but Ability slots A1 and A2 are permanently sealed.
  • Reversion — free automatic RCT every 2 turns restores a captured piece, but Domain slot is sealed.
⚔ Domain Clash Check-Win

When two domains clash, normal effects are suppressed and a 20-turn countdown begins. If either king is put in check during a clash, the side that delivered the check wins — the losing domain collapses instantly, and the winning domain takes full control of the board, resuming all its normal effects.

☸ Megumi (Awakened) — ELO 1200

Megumi (Awakened) uses the Ten Shadows Technique in its most devastating form:

  • Nue (120 CE) — Summon the winged shikigami Nue onto the board. Nue is a Bishop that can teleport to any empty square on the board. It cannot capture or interact with the King in any way. Once destroyed, it cannot be re-summoned. Use again to retract it.
  • Chimera Shadow Garden (500 CE) — Domain Expansion. Each turn, a shadow clone of one of the opponent's pieces appears on the board as a new Black piece. Active for 10 turns. Collapses against Infinite Void and Malevolent Shrine. Clashes equally with TML, SEP, and TCMP.
  • Revival Mechanic — When Megumi's King is captured or checkmated, he does not lose. Instead, the screen displays "With this treasure, I summon..." and Divine General Mahoraga appears as the sole Black piece. All remaining Black pieces vanish.

During the Mahoraga phase: You must either capture Mahoraga to win immediately, or survive 40 turns for Megumi to bleed out. The log shows how many turns remain each round. Mahoraga will never blunder — it only captures when safe. Defeat Megumi (Awakened) to unlock the Mahoraga skill.

⛩ Ryomen Sukuna Heian (ELO 4000 — MAX BOSS)
  • Imaginary Fierce God (Passive) — Heian Sukuna starts with an extra row of pieces R N B Q Q B N R placed in front of his pawns (row 2). These move and capture as normal chess pieces. Equip this reward to gain the same front row for yourself.
  • Malevolent Shrine: Heian (Domain) — The true form of Malevolent Shrine. Destroys the 3 most valuable enemy pieces every 2 turns instead of 2 pieces every 3. Strikes are animated as Heian Cleave → Dismantle → Heian Cleave.
  • Heian Cleave — Auto-destroys the 2 most valuable enemy pieces simultaneously. No targeting needed.
  • Heian Dismantle — Destroys all enemy pieces in a 5-wide horizontal row (c−2 to c+2). Requires column line-of-sight (player), AI ignores this requirement.
  • World Cutting Slash (WCS) — A 4-turn incantation that cannot be stopped. Each turn Sukuna announces a stage as a big screen overlay (Scale of the Dragon → Recoil → Twin Meteors) without ending his chess move. On turn 4 World Cutting Slash fires: the closest enemy piece in every column is destroyed simultaneously. Equip WCS to use the same mechanic — activate it 4 times, each advancing the chant without ending your turn; the 4th fires the attack.
  • Fuga — Requires an active Malevolent Shrine: Heian domain. Destroys all enemy pieces (except King and Mahoraga) in a 5×5 area centered on the selected square. Sukuna auto-targets the densest cluster. As a player skill, click the center square after activating.
  • Rewards on victory — World Cutting Slash, Heian Cleave, Heian Dismantle, Imaginary Fierce God, and Fuga all unlock simultaneously.
🔧 Key Mechanics
  • All rewards unlock at once — Defeating a bot unlocks all of their skills simultaneously.
  • Gojo Sensei uses Six Eyes (50% CE discount) and drops Six Eyes on defeat.
  • Hollow Purple (Index 26) — 1-click: click any column to annihilate it. Annihilation vow adds ±1 adjacent columns (3 total).
  • Mahoraga immunity — Only chess captures, Hollow Purple, and Fuga can destroy Mahoraga. All other skill attacks skip it.
  • Mahoraga adaptation — Adapts to Limitless/Infinity after 3 blocks. Does not adapt to RCT. Cannot be re-summoned after destruction.
  • Cursed Speech — Once per battle, seals for 20 moves (not 3 turns).
  • Lapse Blue — Bypasses Infinity and Limitless entirely. Can capture enemy or own pieces at the destination.
  • Domain Clash — When two domains activate simultaneously, both sides are sealed for 20 turns. Check the enemy King to end the clash early.
🏆 Progression
  • Defeat opponents to unlock all of their techniques at once in the Technique Archive.
  • Techniques carry over between battles — build your kit over time.
  • Harder opponents (higher ELO) drop rarer and more powerful techniques.
  • Reset Progress wipes all unlocks if you want to start fresh.
💡 Tips
  • Save CE for the right moment — spending it all early leaves you defenceless.
  • Domain Clash favours the better chess player. Use check-win to resolve it early.
  • Mahoraga counters domain-heavy opponents like Gojo or Mahito.
  • Against Megumi (Awakened): position to capture Mahoraga quickly after the revival, or build a fortress to survive 40 turns.
  • Hollow Purple is devastating but telegraphed — use it when the enemy's columns are stacked.
  • Infinity + a strong chess position = near-unbreakable defence for one turn.
  • Nue can be used offensively to block key squares or defensively to teleport to safety.
